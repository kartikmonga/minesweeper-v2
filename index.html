How to Play Minesweeper

Objective: Clear the grid without detonating any hidden mines.

  • Click on cells to reveal what's underneath.
  • Right-click (or long-tap) to flag suspected mines.
  • Numbers tell you how many mines are nearby.
  • Hitting a mine ends the game.
